About Us

We are an international student recruitment agency located in Toronto. We recruit for over 40 different educational institutions across Canada.

Services to Our Clients

Marketing

Services to Our Students

At ISRC, we care about our students, their well-being, and success. Here are some of the services we offer them, free of charge.
language

Immigration We provide high-quality, professional immigration advice to our students via our in-house ICCRC certified immigration consultant, including completing and submitting visa and study permit applications.

article

Applications We complete applications to our partner schools for our students, ensuring correctness, accuracy, and completeness of information, including translation and certification of documents.

Scholarships On behalf of our students, we apply to generous financial scholarships, maintain their scholarship file, and request extensions and renewals.
